Discover Tunisia: Essential Travel Information

Everything you need to know before visiting Tunisia - from geography and culture to practical travel tips

Capital & Government

Capital City: Tunis (Population: ~2.7 million metro area)

Government: Unitary semi-presidential republic

National Holiday: March 20 - Independence Day (1956)

Time Zone & Business Hours

Time Zone: GMT+1

  • Shops: 9:00-13:00, 15:00-19:00
  • Banks: 8:00-15:00 (Mon-Fri)
  • Restaurants: 12:00-15:00, 19:00-23:00

Geography & Territory

Total Area: 163,155 km²

Coastline: 1,298 km along the Mediterranean Sea

Bordered by Algeria, Libya, and the Mediterranean

Climate & Weather

Mediterranean climate in the north, semi-arid inland, and Saharan desert in the south

Average Temperatures:

Winter (Dec): 11.4°C • Summer (Jul): 29.3°C

Population & Demographics

Total Population: 11,972,169 (INS 2024)

  • 50.7% women
  • 49.3% men
  • Urban population: ~70%
  • Median age: 33 years

Telephone & Internet

Country Code: +216

  • Tunisie Télécom
  • Ooredoo
  • Orange Tunisia

Currency & Banking

Currency: Tunisian Dinar (TND or DT)

Exchange Rate: 1 EUR ≈ 3.43 TND | 1 USD ≈ 3.15 TND

Transportation & Driving

Driving Side: Right-hand side

Speed limits: Urban 50 km/h, Rural 90 km/h, Highways 110 km/h

International Airports & Airlines

  • Tunis-Carthage
  • Enfidha-Hammamet
  • Monastir-Habib Bourguiba
  • Djerba-Zarzis
  • Tunisair - tunisair.com.tn
  • Nouvelair - nouvelair.com

Languages Spoken

  • Arabic (Official, Tunisian dialect)
  • French (business & education)
  • English (tourism)
  • Italian (some regions)

Religion & Culture

  • Islam - 98%
  • Judaism - ~1,500
  • Christianity - Small minority

Cultural note: Tunisia is known for its religious tolerance and Mediterranean traditions

Water & Food Safety

Bottled water recommended for tourists; eat at popular restaurants and wash fruits

Health & Vaccinations

  • Routine vaccinations up to date
  • Hepatitis A & B recommended
  • Travel insurance strongly advised
